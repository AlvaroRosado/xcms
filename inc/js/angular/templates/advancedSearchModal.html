How to create filters?

You can make custom filters adding(+)/removing(-) filter at the left side of this window. Then click on the SEARCH button to search nodes using the actual filter.

Can I save my custom filters?

Yes, you have to click on the save button and enter a name for the filter. You can also delete it clicking on the trash button.
